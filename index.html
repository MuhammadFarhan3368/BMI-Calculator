What is BMI

Body mass index (BMI) is a tool that healthcare providers use to estimate the amount of body fat by using your height and weight measurements.

The body mass index (BMI) range for obesity is 30 or greater:

  • Underweight: BMI is less than 18.5
  • Healthy weight: BMI is 18.5 to less than 25
  • Overweight: BMI is 25 to less than 30
  • Obesity: BMI is 30 or greater
